Your privacy at a glance

Hello. We are Reel Travel developers. Here's a summary of how we protect your data and respect your privacy.

Types of data we collect

  • Contact details
  • Financial information
  • Data from your contracts
  • Data that identifies you
  • Data on how you use Juro

‍How we use your data

  • To keep website running
  • To Help us improve website
  • To give personalized customer support
  • To send you marketing messages (but only if you tell us to)
  • To comply with laws and regulations and our legal obligations

‍Third parties who process your data‍

The following services help us keep Juro running by storingor processing your data on our behalf:

  • Infrastructure: AWS
  • Analytics: Google Analytics
  • Integrations: (by your request) Google,

    social media platforms or travel booking sites

We use cookies

  • We use only necessary cookies to run and improve the service
  • Our third-party service providers use cookies too (which they control)
  • You can turn off cookies but this will mean that we can't recognize you in in-app messaging and we can't resolve issues as easily.

‍When and how we collect data

The following services help us keep website running by storingor processing your data on our behalf:

  • you register for an account on our website,
  • we store this information securely
  • You browse any page of our website
  • we collect your browsing activity data
  • we may collect your general location information
  • we provide you with the most relevant travel maps
  • you choose to create travel itineraries, save locations, or contribute reviews
  • we store this information to enhance your experience and help other users.

Know your rights

  • Access information we hold on you
  • Opt-out of marketing comms
  • Port your data to another service
  • Complain about us
